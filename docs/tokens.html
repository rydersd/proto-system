Design Tokens

The color palette, typography, and spacing system. Never hardcode hex values.

Rule: Always use var(--wf-token-name) instead of hex values. Tokens automatically adapt across fidelity modes.

Core Palette

TokenSwatchDefaultUse
--wf-ink #1e2a3a Headings, borders, primary text emphasis
--wf-text #3b4f68 Body text, default content
--wf-muted #4a5f7f Secondary text, labels, captions
--wf-line #b0bdd0 Borders, dividers, separator lines
--wf-tint #dce4ef Subtle fills, hover backgrounds
--wf-surface #edf1f7 Card backgrounds, panel fills
--wf-canvas #f0f4fa Page background
--wf-accent #3d6daa The ONE blue — links, primary actions, active states

Semantic Colors

TokenSwatchDefaultUse
--wf-red #8b4553 Errors, overdue, danger actions
--wf-amber #6b5a2f Warnings, pending states
--wf-green #45785a Success, confirmed, positive
--wf-purple #6b5b8a AI features, suggestions

Paper Effects

TokenUse
--wf-paper-shadow Multi-layer shadow for paper depth. Applied to cards.
--wf-tape-color Tape strip fill — rgba(220, 228, 200, 0.55)
--wf-pin-color Pushpin dot — #c0392b
--wf-wobble-radius Imperfect border-radius. Varies by fidelity.
--wf-wobble-filter SVG displacement filter. Napkin: none. Blueprint: url(#wf-line-wobble).
--wf-grain-opacity Paper texture overlay opacity.
--wf-grid-opacity Background grid line opacity.
